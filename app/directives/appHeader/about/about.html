iMAPS 3.0

iMAPS is Wake County's interactive property mapping web application. It is a collaborative effort between Wake County and the City of Raleigh.

iMAPS was developed fully in house by a City of Raleigh GIS employee. It was developed using a combination of open source technology and Esri GIS technology. The code is open source and available on GitHub for reuse.

iMAPS is comprised of numerous datasets, from numerous sources and is made publicly available by Wake County and the City of Raleigh. Most of the datasets are available for download from Wake County and the City of Raleigh's Open GIS Data sites.
